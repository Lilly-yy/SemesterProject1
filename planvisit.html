Getting here

Parking

Free on-site parking is available for all visitors. Designated spaces are provided for disabled badge holders near the main entrance.

Public Transport

Our museum is conveniently located near the downtown area and is accessible by bus and train services. The closest bus stops are just a short walk away, and the Central Station is a 5-minute walk to our main entrance.

Amenities and Services

Restrooms

Clean and accessible restrooms are located on every floor. Each restroom includes a baby changing facility.

Nursing and Baby Care:

We provide private nursing areas for your convenience, equipped with comfortable seating and changing tables. Look for the "Family Care" signs, or ask any staff member for directions.

Interactive Spots for Kids

Our museum is a hands-on learning environment with interactive exhibits scattered throughout. From fossil digs to a physics playground, your little ones will have the opportunity to learn through play.

Cafeteria

The Green Leaf Cafeteria offers a range of healthy and delicious options, including allergy-friendly choices. Indoor and outdoor seating is available so you can enjoy your meal with a view of the museum gardens.

Additional amenities

  • Free Wi-Fi access throughout the museum.
  • Wheelchairs available at no extra cost.
  • A gift shop filled with educational toys and mementos.
  • Personal lockers to store your belongings.

Tips for your visit

  • Wear comfortable shoes, as our exhibit halls span wide areas.
  • Check our event calendar online to catch special exhibits and presentations.
  • Download our mobile app for an interactive map and tour guide. Scan the code below to download the app

Guided tours

There are guided tours of the museum that leave every hour. These tours are 70 NOK per person and include a handy printed guide of the museum.

Group visits

Planning to visit in a group? We offer guided tours for educational trips, special interest groups, and more. Contact us to arrange a personalized experience.

Accessibility

The Bridgemouth Valley Science Museum is committed to being accessible to all. Our facilities are wheelchair friendly, and we offer special programs for visitors with additional needs.
