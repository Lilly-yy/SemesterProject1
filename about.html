About us

Welcome to Bridgemouth Valley Science Museum!

Founded on the pillars of curiosity and discovery, the Bridgemouth Valley Science Museum is a beacon of knowledge that illuminates the wonders of our universe.

Opening its doors in 2024, our museum is a cutting-edge facility dedicated to exploring the depths of cosmology, the intricacies of evolution, the marvels of biology and medicine, the innovation of robotics and AI, and the vital importance of ecology.

At Bridgemouth Valley Science Museum, we believe in igniting the spark of curiosity that resides in every visitor.

Our interactive exhibits, educational programs, and community events are designed to inspire and educate visitors of all ages. Many of our exhibits are designed for you to interact with and play around to see science come to life (apart from the dinosaur exhibits – they only come to life at night when everyone’s gone home).

Join us on a journey through science where every visit expands your horizon.

Your adventure into the unknown awaits!
